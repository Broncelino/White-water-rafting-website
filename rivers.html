Snake River info

The snake river is the largest tributary to the Columbia river at just over 1750 miles long. It begins in western Wyoming and flows all the way to Washington where it flows into the Columbia River.

Owyhee River

The Owyhee River starts in north east Nevada and flows north along the east side of the Independence mountains. It then goes through the south east corner of Idaho then to into Oregon where it tributes into the Snake river.

Selway River info

The Selway River is a tributary of the Clearwater River with the main stem spanning about 100 miles. It orriginates from within Bitterroot National Forrest.
